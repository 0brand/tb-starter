Unofficial I2P Browser

This browser does not enable isolation of connections by domain or by browser tab. This means that your identity is the same in every browser tab you are using. If a person owns two sites, and you visit both of those sites at the same time, it is possible for them to know. Keep this in mind when you browse I2P.
